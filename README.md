## üèóÔ∏è Database Architecture

### **Phase 1: Conceptual Design**
- **Entity-Relationship Modeling**: Comprehensive ER diagrams defining core business entities
- **Business Rule Analysis**: Translation of travel industry requirements into database constraints
- **Entity Identification**: Customers, Tours, Vendors, Bookings, Payments, Services

### **Phase 2: Logical Design**
- **Enhanced ER Diagrams (EERD)**: Advanced relationship modeling with inheritance and specialization
- **Normalization**: Applied 1NF, 2NF, and 3NF principles for optimal data organization
- **Relationship Mapping**: Detailed foreign key relationships and cardinality definitions

### **Phase 3: Physical Implementation**
- **SQL Server Database**: Production-ready schema with optimized data types
- **Table Structures**: 15+ interconnected tables supporting complex business operations
- **Constraints & Integrity**: Primary keys, foreign keys, check constraints, and business rules

### **Phase 4: Advanced Database Features**
- **Stored Procedures**: Automated booking processes, payment handling, and data validation
- **Triggers**: Real-time inventory updates, audit trails, and business rule enforcement
- **Indexing Strategy**: Performance optimization for high-frequency queries
- **Data Security**: Encryption implementation for sensitive customer and payment data
